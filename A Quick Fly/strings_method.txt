------------------- Strings Methods in Python -------------------


1. title 	-> 	capitalize word first letter
2. lower 	-> 	convert the text in lower case
3. upper 	-> 	convert the text in upper case
4. rstrip 	-> 	remove whitespace from right
5. lstrip 	-> 	remove whitespace from right
6. strip  	->	remove whitespace from right and left
7. capitalize	->	capitalize the first word in the text
